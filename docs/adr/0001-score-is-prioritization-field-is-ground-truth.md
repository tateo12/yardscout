# Score is a prioritization, not a guarantee; the field visit is ground truth

Viability (Score) is computed purely from free parcel geometry — open space (parcel area minus building footprint) and side-yard clearance for Access. No dataset can confirm Access for certain (fences, sheds, gates, slope, meters are not in the data), and we deliberately reject a human desk pre-screen of the greens as too slow for a small family operation. Therefore the green Score drives the knock list directly, and the Field visit is treated as the only real verification of Access. Each Knock records the confirmed result, which removes false-positive greens from the list permanently and ground-truths the map over a season.

Consequence: the team will occasionally drive to a green that turns out blocked. We accept that cost in exchange for zero pre-screening labor and a map that self-corrects from real visits.
